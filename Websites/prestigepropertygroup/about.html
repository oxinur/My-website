About Us

Our Story

London's Premier Estate Agency Since 2009

Established in 2009, Prestige Property Group has become one of London's most trusted and respected estate agencies. From our offices in Grosvenor Square, Mayfair, we offer an unrivalled service to buyers, sellers, landlords, and tenants seeking exceptional residential property across prime Central London.

Our philosophy is simple: exceptional properties deserve exceptional service. Whether you are purchasing your first London home, expanding your investment portfolio, or seeking the perfect rental, our dedicated consultants are with you every step of the journey.

  • Over 2,400 prime properties sold since 2009
  • 97% client satisfaction rating across all services
  • Regulated by the Property Ombudsman
  • Specialist knowledge across all prime London postcodes
