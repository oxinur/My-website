Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

Q: How do I register with Prestige Property Group to view properties?

A: Simply complete our online registration form or call our Grosvenor Square office on +44 (0)20 7946 0821. One of our consultants will arrange a free initial consultation to understand your requirements and match you with the most suitable properties.

Q: Which areas of London do you specialise in?

A: We specialise in prime and super-prime London locations including Mayfair, Belgravia, Kensington, Chelsea, Notting Hill, St John's Wood, and Canary Wharf. Our consultants have deep, first-hand knowledge of each neighbourhood.

Q: Do you offer lettings as well as residential sales?

A: Yes. We provide a full lettings and property management service alongside our residential and commercial sales. Our lettings team manages over 200 properties across prime London and handles everything from tenant-finding to full property management.

Q: What is included in your property management service?

A: Our comprehensive management service includes tenant finding, thorough referencing, tenancy agreement preparation, rent collection, maintenance coordination, regular property inspections, annual rent reviews, and full compliance with current UK lettings legislation.

Q: Can you assist with overseas investment purchases?

A: Absolutely. We have considerable experience assisting international clients with UK property investment, including guidance on mortgage options for non-UK residents, taxation implications, the legal conveyancing process, and ongoing portfolio management.

Q: How do you value my property?

A: We offer a free, no-obligation market appraisal conducted by one of our senior consultants. We use live market comparable data, recent sales evidence, and our extensive local knowledge to provide an accurate and honest valuation — never inflated to win your instruction.
