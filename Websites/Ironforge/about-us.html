About IronForge

Built for Champions Since 2012

Founded in Manchester in 2012, IronForge Fitness Club has grown from a single training floor into a 10,000 sq ft premium facility. We've helped over 5,000 members achieve real, lasting results — whether that's losing weight, building muscle, training for competition, or simply feeling better every day. Our team of qualified coaches brings decades of expertise across strength and conditioning, nutrition, yoga, boxing, and rehabilitation.
