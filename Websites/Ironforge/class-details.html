Olympic Weightlifting

Olympic Weightlifting at IronForge Fitness develops total-body strength, explosive power, and technical precision. Our dedicated lifting platforms and Level 4 certified coaches make this one of our most popular and rewarding disciplines, suitable for beginners through to competitive athletes.

Your Coach

All IronForge classes are led by fully accredited Level 3 or Level 4 coaches. Our team combines academic knowledge with real competitive experience, so you receive coaching grounded in science and sharpened by practice. Every session includes proper warm-up, technical instruction, coached working sets, and cool-down.

Emily Thornton

Olympic Weightlifting Coach

Emily holds a BSc in Sport & Exercise Science and has competed at national level in Olympic weightlifting. She's coached over 200 athletes across all abilities and is passionate about making the sport accessible to everyone.
