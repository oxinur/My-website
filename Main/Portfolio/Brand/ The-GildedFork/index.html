About Us

Welcome to The Gilded Fork

Nestled in the heart of Marylebone since 1986, The Gilded Fork has built a reputation for delivering extraordinary British fine dining experiences. Our kitchen team, led by Executive Chef William Harrison, sources the finest seasonal produce from trusted British farms and coastal fisheries, transforming outstanding ingredients into dishes that celebrate the very best of these islands. Every visit is a journey — from the first amuse-bouche to the last petit four.

The Foundation of Excellence

Provenance & Seasonal Ingredients

We believe great cooking starts long before the kitchen. Every ingredient on our menu is sourced from trusted British producers — from Hereford beef aged for 28 days, to day-boat fish from Cornwall's coast, to seasonal vegetables grown on family farms in the Home Counties. We partner directly with our suppliers, visiting their farms and harbours to understand what true quality means. When we know who grew it, we cook it better.
