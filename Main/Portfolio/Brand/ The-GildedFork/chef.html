Meet the Chefs

William Harrison

Executive Chef & Founder

With over 25 years' experience in Michelin-starred kitchens, William founded The Gilded Fork in 1986 to celebrate the finest British seasonal produce. His signature is elegance through simplicity.

Charlotte Pemberton

Head Chef

Trained at Le Cordon Bleu in London and Lyons, Charlotte brings classical French technique and modern British sensibility to every plate she creates. She champions sustainable sourcing throughout the menu.

Ethan Blackwood

Sous Chef

Ethan's mastery of fire and fermentation brings extraordinary depth and complexity to our dinner menu. His signature duck confit has become a true Gilded Fork institution, beloved by regulars and critics alike.

Priya Nair

Pastry Chef

Priya trained at L'École Nationale Supérieure de Pâtisserie in Paris and brings an exquisite, jewel-like precision to our dessert menu, balancing British tradition with contemporary creativity.

Our Story

Welcome to The Gilded Fork

Founded by William Harrison in 1986, The Gilded Fork was born from a conviction that British fine dining could be world-class. Over nearly four decades, our kitchen team has grown into one of London's most celebrated brigades — united by a shared passion for provenance, craft, and the simple joy of extraordinary food. From breakfast through to late evening dinner, every dish we serve represents the very best of our team's skill and dedication.

The Gilded Fork

Award-winning fine dining at the heart of Marylebone, London. The Gilded Fork brings together the finest British seasonal ingredients and classical technique for an unforgettable dining experience since 1986.
