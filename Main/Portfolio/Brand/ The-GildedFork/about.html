Our Philosophy

Rooted in Provenance & Craft

The Gilded Fork was founded in 1986 by William Harrison with a single, uncompromising belief: that great food begins with great ingredients. Situated in the elegant surroundings of Marylebone, we have spent nearly four decades building relationships with the finest British farmers, fishermen, and artisan producers. Our menus change with the seasons, driven entirely by what the land and sea give us at their best.

From day-boat fish landed on the Cornish coast to Herefordshire beef aged for 28 days in our own dry-ageing chamber, every ingredient that enters our kitchen has a story. We believe that story belongs on the plate, cooked with skill, served with warmth, and enjoyed with the people you love.
