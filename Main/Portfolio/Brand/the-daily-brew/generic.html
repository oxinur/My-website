We believe in the transformative power of a perfect cup

The Daily Brew was founded on the belief that coffee is more than just a beverage — it's a ritual, a moment of connection, and a celebration of craftsmanship. We source our beans from trusted farmers across the globe, roast them fresh every week, and serve each cup with passion.

How The Daily Brew Began

The Daily Brew was founded in 2018 by siblings Priya and Daniel Ashworth, who shared a lifelong passion for exceptional coffee and a belief that Manchester deserved a speciality café that went beyond the ordinary. After years of working with coffee farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, they returned to their home city with a van full of green beans, a refurbished Probat roaster, and an uncompromising vision of what a neighbourhood café could be.

From the outset, we built relationships directly with the smallholder farms that supply our beans. Every origin on our menu has been visited by a member of our team. We pay a substantial premium above fair-trade pricing, invest in on-farm infrastructure, and share full traceability data with our customers — right down to the altitude at which the coffee was grown and the processing method used. This direct-trade approach means the flavours in your cup tell a genuine story, not a marketing one.

Our café sits in the heart of Manchester's Northern Quarter, in a converted Victorian warehouse with exposed brick walls, reclaimed timber tables, and natural light flooding through tall arched windows. We roast our coffee fresh every week in small batches, and our espresso bar is designed to let you watch the whole process — from weighing the dose to watching the extraction. Our baristas are trained to industry competition standard, and we actively encourage questions. Whether you are exploring coffee for the first time or chasing a specific tasting note, we will brew you something that genuinely excites us.

Beyond the espresso bar, The Daily Brew runs a monthly coffee school offering beginner home-brewing workshops, barista skills courses, and cupping sessions open to the public. We also supply our freshly roasted beans to a growing list of independent restaurants, boutique hotels, and workplace kitchens across the North West. If you are passionate about great coffee, we would love to welcome you — whether as a customer, a student, or a wholesale partner.
