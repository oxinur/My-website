Key Terms

Single Origin

Coffee sourced from one specific farm, cooperative, or region. Single-origin coffees allow you to taste the distinct flavour characteristics shaped by the local climate, soil, and processing method of a single place.

Direct Trade

A purchasing model in which a roastery buys directly from the producer, bypassing commodity brokers. This ensures better prices for farmers, greater supply chain transparency, and tighter quality control for the roaster.

Washed Process

A coffee processing method in which the cherry fruit is removed before the bean is dried. This results in a cleaner, brighter cup with pronounced acidity and clarity of origin flavour — the hallmark of many Ethiopian and Kenyan coffees.
