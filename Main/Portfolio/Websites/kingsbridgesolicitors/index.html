Welcome to Kingsbridge Solicitors

Delivering Justice and Results for Our Clients

Established in 1983, Kingsbridge Solicitors LLP has built a reputation as one of London's most trusted law firms. Our solicitors are known for their sharp legal minds, commercial acumen, and genuine commitment to client outcomes.

Whether you are navigating a complex corporate transaction, protecting your family's future, defending against criminal charges, or resolving a property dispute, our specialist teams are here to guide you at every step.

We are proud to be authorised and regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority, operating from our offices in Chancery Lane — at the heart of London's legal district.

Our Areas of Legal Expertise

Family Law

Expert guidance on divorce, child arrangements, financial settlements, and all aspects of family breakdown. Empathetic, results-focused representation from £850 + VAT.

Corporate Law

Full-service corporate legal support including mergers, acquisitions, shareholder agreements, and company restructuring. Fixed-fee packages available from £1,200 + VAT.

Insurance Law

Representing policyholders and insurers in coverage disputes, professional indemnity, and personal injury claims. No-win no-fee options on qualifying cases.

Criminal Defence

Robust defence representation at all levels of court, from Magistrates to Crown Court. 24-hour emergency advice line available. Legally aided cases accepted.

Property Law

Residential and commercial conveyancing, lease negotiations, landlord and tenant disputes. Competitive fixed-fee conveyancing from £650 + VAT + disbursements.

Employment Law

Advising both employers and employees on unfair dismissal, discrimination, TUPE, and redundancy. Employment Tribunal representation from £750 + VAT.

Civil Litigation

Handling high-value commercial disputes, breach of contract, injunctions, and debt recovery. Experienced before the High Court and Court of Appeal.

Financial Law

FCA regulatory compliance, financial services agreements, fraud recovery, and investment dispute resolution. Authoritative advice from qualified specialists.
