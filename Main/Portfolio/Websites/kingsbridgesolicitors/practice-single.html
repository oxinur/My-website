Family Law Overview

Our Family Law team at Kingsbridge Solicitors understands that family matters are among the most emotionally demanding situations a person can face. Whether you are contemplating divorce, disputing arrangements for your children, or need urgent protection from a partner, we combine legal expertise with genuine compassion to guide you through every step.

We advise on the full spectrum of family law issues, from straightforward uncontested divorces to highly complex, high-net-worth financial remedy proceedings. Our solicitors appear in the Family Court and the Family Division of the High Court, and regularly act in cases involving trusts, offshore assets, and business interests.

Our Family Law Services

We offer comprehensive legal support across all family law matters, including: divorce and dissolution of civil partnerships; financial remedy orders and consent orders; child arrangements orders (residence and contact); prohibited steps and specific issue orders; domestic abuse injunctions and non-molestation orders; cohabitation agreements; pre- and post-nuptial agreements; and international family law matters.

Our fees are transparent and clearly communicated from the outset. Uncontested divorces start from £850 + VAT. For contested financial proceedings, we offer fixed-fee stages wherever possible, so you retain control of your legal costs throughout.
