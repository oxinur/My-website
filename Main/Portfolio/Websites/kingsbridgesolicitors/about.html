Our Story

Over Four Decades of Legal Excellence

Founded in 1983 by senior partner Gerald Kingsbridge, the firm began as a boutique practice on Chancery Lane specialising in commercial property. Over the following decades, we expanded our expertise across every major discipline of English and Welsh law.

Today, Kingsbridge Solicitors LLP employs over 60 solicitors, barristers, and legal executives working across eight specialist departments. We act for private individuals, entrepreneurs, SMEs, and publicly listed companies throughout England and Wales — and, where required, internationally through our network of partner firms.

Our core values are straightforward: give clear, honest advice; act with integrity at all times; and fight tenaciously for every client's best outcome. We are proud that over 65% of our new instructions come from existing clients and personal referrals — a measure we regard as our finest endorsement.
