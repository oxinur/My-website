Notting Hill, London W11 3QE — Leasehold, 978 years remaining

Property Description

An immaculate and beautifully presented lateral apartment arranged on the first floor of a handsome stucco-fronted Victorian period building, situated moments from Notting Hill Gate underground station and the vibrant boutiques and restaurants of Westbourne Grove.

The property has been the subject of an extensive and sympathetic renovation by the current owners, retaining many original features including high ceilings, ornate cornicing and wide period floorboards, whilst introducing a thoroughly modern specification throughout. The accommodation is arranged across an impressive 1,850 square feet and comprises a superb open-plan reception room and kitchen diner, four generous double bedrooms — the principal with a full en-suite bathroom — a stylish family bathroom and excellent storage throughout.

The kitchen is fitted to an exceptional standard with bespoke cabinetry, integrated Gaggenau appliances, and a large central island. There is further access to a private courtyard garden to the rear of the building, exclusive to the first-floor apartment.
