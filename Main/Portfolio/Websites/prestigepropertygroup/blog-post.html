Why Now Is the Right Time to Invest in Prime London Property

By James Harrington, Head of Sales — 12 April 2026

Despite broader economic headwinds and uncertainty surrounding interest rate trajectories, prime central London property continues to demonstrate its resilience as a long-term investment asset. After a period of repricing through 2024 and early 2025, we are now seeing clear evidence that values have stabilised and that carefully selected acquisitions represent compelling value for sophisticated investors.

Supply Remains Exceptionally Constrained

The fundamental driver underpinning prime London values is a chronic shortage of high-quality stock. Planning restrictions, listed building constraints and the sheer difficulty of building new prime residential properties in central postcodes mean that supply will never meaningfully increase. Yet demand from ultra-high-net-worth buyers — particularly from the Middle East, South Asia and North America — continues to grow.

International Buyers Are Returning

Following the post-pandemic surge in domestic demand, the international buyer has returned in force to the prime London market. Sterling's relative weakness against the dollar and dirham makes UK property attractively priced for dollar-pegged and dollar-influenced buyers, who now account for a significant proportion of transactions above £3 million in Mayfair and Belgravia.

Rental Returns Are at Multi-Year Highs

For investors considering a buy-to-let strategy, gross rental yields in prime London have risen to their highest level since 2008, with some Canary Wharf and City fringe properties achieving yields north of 5%. Tenant demand has never been stronger, driven by a growing corporate relocation market and the continued appeal of London as a global financial and cultural centre.

Our Recommendation

At Prestige Property Group, we advise clients to take a long view. Prime London property purchased at current values, in the best locations and buildings, has consistently outperformed over every 10-year period since records began. The window of opportunity at current pricing may be relatively brief — we encourage any buyers who have been waiting on the sidelines to register with our team without delay.
