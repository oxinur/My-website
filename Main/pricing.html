FAQs

Common questions

How long does a website take to build?
Most websites are completed within 2–4 weeks, depending on the package and how quickly you can provide content and feedback. E-commerce projects typically take 3–6 weeks.
Do I need to provide content?
We'll guide you on what's needed. You'll provide the key information about your business, and we can help with copywriting and sourcing images if required.
What if I want to make changes later?
All our Professional and E-Commerce websites are built with HTML and CSS, making it easy to manage later.
Is hosting included in the price?
Hosting is not included in the one-off price, but we offer reliable UK hosting from £49.99/month which includes security updates, backups, and ongoing support.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. We typically take a 50% deposit to start and the remainder on completion. For larger projects we can split payments into three stages — just ask.
